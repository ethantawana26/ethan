Frequently Asked Questions

How does fare negotiation work?
You propose a price, and drivers can accept, reject, or counter-offer. This inDrive-style system ensures fairness for both parties.
How do I register as a driver?
Register in-app under "I am a Driver". Provide your vehicle details and license. For inDrive registration visit indrive.com/en-zw/driver/
Which currencies are accepted?
USD (primary) and RTGS/ZiG. Tap&Go supports cashless payments across all routes.
What is ZUPCO?
ZUPCO (Zimbabwe United Passenger Company) is the state-owned bus operator. Routes cover Harare, Bulawayo, and major towns.
