Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We collect your name, email, phone number, and transit usage data solely to provide personalised transport services. Payment data is processed by PCI-DSS certified partners and never stored in our system.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to: manage your transit account, generate your personal QR pass, improve route suggestions, and send security notifications. We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

3. Encryption & Security

All personal data is encrypted at rest. Passwords are hashed using a secure one-way algorithm before storage. Your QR token is cryptographically generated and cannot be reverse-engineered to reveal personal information.

4. QR Code Security

Your personal QR code contains an encrypted session token. It expires and can be regenerated at any time from your dashboard. Never share your QR code with unknown parties.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, export, or permanently delete your data at any time through the Security section of your dashboard.

6. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication only. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used.

7. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your membership plus 30 days after deletion for legal compliance. Transit logs are anonymised after 90 days.
