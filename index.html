ℹ️ ABOUT RHS TRANSPORT

RHS Transport is Zimbabwe's #1 E-Transport Hub connecting riders with ZUPCO buses, kombis, motorbike taxis, regular taxis, and ride-hailing services like inDrive and Bolt.

Our mission is to provide the fastest, greenest, and cheapest transport options across all major Zimbabwean cities including Harare, Bulawayo, Mutare, Gweru, Masvingo, Victoria Falls, and Kwekwe.

Revenue Model: We charge a low 10-12% commission — lower than competitors — ensuring drivers earn more and riders pay less.
