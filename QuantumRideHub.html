FREQUENTLY ASKED
How does fare negotiation work? ›
You set your own price offer. Nearby drivers see it and can accept, decline, or counter-offer. You choose which driver to accept — full price transparency, no hidden fees.
Why did my driver call me? ›
After accepting your ride, drivers are encouraged to call and confirm exact pickup details. Phone numbers are always masked — your real number is never shared with the driver.
What payment methods are accepted? ›
Cash (USD/ZWL), EcoCash, Mukuru, Paynow, and in-app wallet. Visa/Mastercard card support coming Q3 2026.
Where does QuantumRide operate? ›
Currently active in Harare, Bulawayo, Mutare, Gweru, and Masvingo. Expanding to 20+ Zimbabwe cities by end of 2026.
How accurate is the live car tracking? ›
Driver GPS updates every 3 seconds via WebSockets from the driver's phone. Car markers animate smoothly between GPS positions using linear interpolation — accuracy is ±4 metres with modern phones.
